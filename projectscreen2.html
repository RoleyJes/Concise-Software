Problem

Creatable is an education technology platform partnering with industry to create inspiring teacher professional development. Creatable's ultimate goal is prepare students for the future of work. Creatable was founded by Greg Attwells and is being incubated by leading production house Finch Company.

Solution Provided

Design of technical architecture for a scalable, customised learning management system with very specific requirements, based on the client's existing Figma User Interface designs for desktop and mobile. Implementation in Bubble.io, testing, deployment and support.

Conclusion and Next Steps

Creatable conducted a closed Beta test in March 2021 with the course 'Collaborative Problem Solving' featuring senior team members from Atlassian. They have further developed their product and have just launched 'Version 1.0' in August 2021. They are planning to test the product further in the Australian market in 2021, develop several more courses and launch to the US in 2022.
